### Hello, world! 👋

I am a PhD student at the [Data Science for Humanity Group](https://www.mpi-sp.org/cha) of [MPI-SP](https://www.mpi-sp.org). 
My research focuses on applied AI for scientific and societal impact, spanning computational social science, AI for social good, representation learning, and generative modeling. 
I am interested in building data-driven, scalable, and explainable AI systems to understand human behavior and support decision-making in real-world settings. 
Within AI for science, I am interested in the generative modeling of protein structures for antigen-conditioned antibody design.
